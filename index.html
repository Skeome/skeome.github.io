About Skeome

I am Skeome, a writer, artist, musician, alchemist, and shamanic practitioner. For the majority of my life, I have been blessed with a deep connection to the spiritual realms. This connection allows me to act as a bridge for those who need guidance and healing, navigating both the seen and unseen worlds.

My path is one of service. I am dedicated to Spirit, to the Earth, and to individuals seeking to understand their journey on a deeper level. Through time-honored shamanic practices and the transformative arts of alchemy, I facilitate experiences that help restore balance, retrieve lost soul parts, and unlock your innate wisdom. This practice is dedicated to raising vibration and fostering profound self-realization.
