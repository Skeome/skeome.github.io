Convert Gardiner sign codes (e.g., A1, G43) or MdC alphabet (e.g., m, n, i) into copyable Unicode Egyptian Hieroglyphs. Unicode 17.0 verified. Built for the Egyptology Research Archive.

V2 — Unicode 17.0 Verified Sign Database with Extended Set A

  • Transliteration Mapping: Recognizes academic diacritics (ꜣ ꜥ ḥ ḫ ẖ š ṯ ḏ). Typing words like nfr or bꜣ pulls the correct sign sequences.
  • Sign-Type Tagging: The phonetic preview now labels each resolved sign as Phon. (phonogram), Det. (determinative), Log. (logogram), or Num. (numeral) — with intelligent grouping of compound sequences.
  • Intelligent Parsing: Understands Gardiner codes (M17, G43), MdC uniliterals (i w t), and phonetic word aliases (nfr, Htp, anx). Handles quadrat stacking with : and horizontal grouping with *.
  • Unicode 17.0 Database: Full coverage of U+13000–U+1342F (1,079 Gardiner signs) plus Hieroglyphs Extended-A (U+13460–U+143FF, 3,382 signs). Input Extended-A by hex code, e.g. EA.13462. All codepoints verified against official Unicode charts.
