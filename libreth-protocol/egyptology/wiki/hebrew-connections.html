VIII. Atenism and the Origins of Hebrew Monotheism

The "Great Hymn to the Aten" found in the tomb of Ay contains passages that are nearly identical to Psalm 104. While the traditions diverged—Atenism focusing on the visible disk, and Hebrew tradition on the invisible creator—the Systemic Re-initialization toward a singular divine source is a shared historical frequency.
