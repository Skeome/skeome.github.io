Institutional Foundations

Modern Egyptology is rooted in the convergence of archaeological rigor and digital accessibility. These repositories provide high-resolution imagery and translated primary sources essential for the Egyptology Research Project.

Museum Collections

The physical evidence of Kemet is preserved in global archives. The British Museum and the Metropolitan Museum of Art offer comprehensive digital catalogs of stelae, statues, and ritual objects.
