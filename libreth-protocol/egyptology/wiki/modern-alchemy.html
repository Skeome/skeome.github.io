The Current Era

The work of the House of Life continues in the modern age through the dedicated efforts of alchemists and initiatory brotherhoods. This is not a revival but a re-activation of the unbroken chain of knowledge connecting Kemet to the present.

Brotherhood and Transmission

Modern alchemy, which can be fundamentally defined as the raising of one's vibrations, maintains an unbroken lineage of knowledge through specialized circles and dedicated researchers. Modern practitioners utilize both spiritual discipline and laboratory practice to manifest higher frequencies of being, emphasizing the mastery of both "wet" (laboratory) and "dry" (internal) paths of transmutation.

Operational Focus

The contemporary alchemist seeks to align their work with the primordial principles of Ma'at (cosmic order). By applying the Hermetic maxim "As above, so below," the practitioner works to harmonize the micro-vibrations of matter with the macro-laws of the universe.

  • Spagyric Medicine: The systematic extraction and recombination of plant essences. This practice seeks to separate the "sulfur," "mercury," and "salt" of a plant to create highly potent remedies that act on multiple levels of the human constitution.
  • Laboratory Metallurgy: The investigation into the properties of metallic oxides and monoatomic elements, continuing the research into the material manifestation of solar power.
  • Internal Transmutation: The use of ritual and meditation to refine the practitioner's own spirit, aiming for the state of the transfigured Akh.
