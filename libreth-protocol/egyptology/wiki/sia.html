The Heart of Creation

Sia represents the personification of perception, intellectual understanding, and divine knowledge. In the Memphite Theology, Sia is the power of the heart—the seat of thought—that conceptualizes the universe before it is manifest.

Sia is the cognitive spark that recognizes the patterns of Ma'at within the primordial chaos of the Awareness Field. Without Sia, the creative force of Heka would have no direction, and the authoritative utterance of Hu would have no content.

Guardian of the Solar Barque

In the Netherworld Books, Sia is one of the two essential companions who stand upon the solar barque of Re. Alongside Heka, Sia provides the sun god with the perception necessary to navigate the twelve hours of the night and defeat the serpent Apep.

Hermetic Correspondence

Sia is the direct Kemetic ancestor of the Hermetic Nous (Divine Mind). It is the faculty of direct spiritual apprehension—Gnosis—that allows the initiate to perceive the underlying unity of the cosmos.
