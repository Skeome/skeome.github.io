Ancient Kemetic inscriptions, particularly at the temple of Serabit el-Khadim, refer to a mysterious substance called mfkȝt. While often translated as "turquoise," the contextual use of the term suggests a refined alchemical product — a White Powder or Potable Gold.

In this research framework, this substance is viewed as a biological superconductor. It was believed to be the "Bread of Presence" or "Bread of the Gods," a material sustenance that permitted the transfigured Akh soul to maintain its stability and power within the material and subtle realms.
